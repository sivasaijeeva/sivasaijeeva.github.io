    • INTERNET: ONLY THE RICHEST SURVIVE
    Opinion • September 9, 2015

    The debate about net neutrality once broke the internet, what remains now is silence. It is nowhere to be heard in the media. Net neutrality is access to all contents on the internet without favouring or blocking specific contents. The question arises – Is net neutrality a fundamental right of the modern age? There are many complex questions which need to be asked and answered. Most youngsters in India use inter- net but the number of people who know about actual Inter- net are very less. You pay for bandwidth (speed) and data (GB). More money for more bandwidth is the scheme which most ISPs follow. This is common for internet as whole but more bandwidth for a specific website and free access to some premium websites like Facebook, Wikipedia and WhatsApp is a new idea put forth by the ISPs.

    The concept of free internet services sounds interesting but it restricts young companies to prosper. When a rich company pays ISPs, its website can be accessed for free. So it is obvious that people go for the freebies.

    DIVE IN DELIGHT! BON APPETITE
    Review • September 1, 2015

    Every food delineates a unique culture, to experience the culture one should always taste its cuisine. If you wish to experience the Kashmiri culture you should savour the Royal multi-course Kashmiri cuisine,Wazwan.

    Brace all your senses because when you enter the Wazwan restaurant, which is named after this luxurious cuisine,the aura and aroma of Kashmir will hit you hard.You will be enchanted by its decor,eyes filled with vermillion and striking celtic ceiling to garnish it. Traditional carved wooden furnitures invites you to the feast of your lifetime while you are awe-struck.
